About Me

Things I adore....

Creating memories

I love my camera with my whole heart and at this point I think my camera is sick of me. No matter where I am or how I am feeling that day, I will somehow always be able to capture a memory with my camera or my phone.

Sugarrrrrr

If you read my banner, you know I can not live without sugar. Between me and whoever is reading this, I am not the biggest fan of caffeine. Sugar is the only thing keeping me from turning into a zombie from 8am to 3pm.

Reading books

I am obsessed with reading. The moment I get my hand on a book it's over for my planned tasks on that day. I read all type of books from chinese web novels to american novels! Currently, I am loving ancient chinese novels.

Building things

A hobby of mine is making fine jewelry. I love the creativity and the endless design options I have when creating a jewelry design. Don't get me wrong, I love being creative and doing things with my hands but making jewelry will probably be the reason why my wallet is empty.
